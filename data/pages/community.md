# Community

eXist-db is developed by a global community of developers, researchers, and organizations. Here's how to get involved.

## Connect

- **GitHub** — [github.com/eXist-db/exist](https://github.com/eXist-db/exist) — source code, issues, pull requests
- **Slack** — [exist-db.slack.com](https://exist-db.slack.com) — real-time chat with developers and users
- **Mailing list** — [exist-open](https://sourceforge.net/projects/exist/lists/exist-open) — discussion list for users and developers
- **Mastodon** — [@existdb@fosstodon.org](https://fosstodon.org/@existdb)

## Community Calls

The eXist-db community holds regular video calls to discuss development, share demos, and coordinate releases. Check the mailing list for announcements.

## Contributing

Contributions are welcome — whether code, documentation, bug reports, or community support. See the [Contributing Guide](https://github.com/eXist-db/exist/blob/develop/CONTRIBUTING.md) for details.

## Organizations Using eXist-db

eXist-db powers applications in digital humanities, government, publishing, and enterprise:

- Office of the Historian, U.S. Department of State
- Tibetan Buddhist Resource Center
- Technische Universitat Darmstadt
- University of Victoria Humanities Computing
- Centre for Scholarly Editing, Royal Academy of Dutch Language and Literature
- University of Heidelberg, Cluster of Excellence "Asia and Europe"

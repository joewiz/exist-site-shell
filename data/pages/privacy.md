# Privacy Policy

## Data Collection

The eXist-db.org website does not collect personal data, use tracking cookies, or employ analytics services.

## Server Logs

Standard web server logs (IP address, requested URL, timestamp, user agent) may be retained for operational purposes and security monitoring. These logs are not shared with third parties.

## Cookies

This site uses session cookies only when you log in to the eXist-db instance. No third-party cookies are set.

## Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact the eXist-db project via [GitHub](https://github.com/eXist-db/exist).
